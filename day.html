About Us

Welcome to Dayspring Church Zambia. We are a community of believers dedicated to making a positive impact in our society through faith and action.

Our mission encompasses helping the poor, engaging in community projects, planting churches, and promoting healing and deliverance. We believe that faith is not just a set of beliefs, but a call to action, and our actions reflect our commitment to these core values:

  • Helping the Poor: We are passionate about reaching out to the less fortunate, providing support, and improving their lives through various initiatives.
  • Community Projects: We actively participate in community development projects that aim to enhance the well-being of our local neighborhoods and communities.
  • Planting Churches: We believe in spreading the message of love, hope, and faith by establishing churches in different regions, bringing people together in worship and fellowship.
  • Healing and Deliverance: We offer spiritual guidance, support, and healing services to those in need, providing a safe space for individuals to find solace and renewal.

Our vision is to spread love, hope, and transformation throughout Zambia and beyond. We are committed to creating a better world by reaching out to those in need and fostering spiritual growth and healing.
